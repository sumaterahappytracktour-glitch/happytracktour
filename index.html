Why Choose Us?

At Sumatera Happy Track Jungle Trekking, where adventure meets purpose in the heart of Bukit Lawang, the gateway to the world-renowned Gunung Leuser National Park—one of the last places on Earth where wild orangutans still roam free. We don’t just offer jungle treks. We create meaningful journeys that connect you with nature, empower local communities, and help protect one of the most important ecosystems in the world. Explore the wild heart of Sumatra Join us on an unforgettable journey through the lush rainforests of Bukit Lawang — home to the magnificent wild orangutans. Our guided treks connect you with nature, local culture, and real conservation efforts that protect this incredible ecosystem. Let’s make every step matter for the forest, wildlife, and community
