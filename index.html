Why Choose Us?

Sumatra Happy Track Tour Jungle Trekking offers authentic and unforgettable jungle trekking experiences in Bukit Lawang, located at the edge of the world-famous Gunung Leuser National Park. Our tours are guided by experienced and friendly local experts who are passionate about nature, wildlife, and conservation. We specialize in eco-friendly trekking, giving you the opportunity to see Sumatran orangutans in their natural habitat, along with other wildlife such as monkeys, gibbons, and exotic birds. We offer a range of trekking options from short jungle walks to multi-day adventures, including overnight stays in the rainforest, traditional riverside camping, and exciting river tubing experiences. At Sumatra Happy Track Tour, we focus on safety, sustainability, and creating a truly personal experience. Our goal is not only to show you the beauty of the jungle, but also to support the local community and protect the environment. Explore the jungle with us – and create memories that last a lifetime.
